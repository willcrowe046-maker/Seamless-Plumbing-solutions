What Counts as a Plumbing Emergency?

If you're unsure, call us — we'll advise you immediately at no charge. Here are the most common emergencies we handle daily.

Burst Pipe

Water gushing from walls, ceiling, or under the floor. Turn off your water at the mains stopcock and call us immediately. We'll be there in under an hour.

No Hot Water

A failed hot water system — especially with children or in winter — is an emergency. We carry common units on the van for same-day replacement.

Overflowing Drain or Toilet

A completely blocked or overflowing toilet or drain poses a health risk and can flood your home. Don't wait — call us now.

Gas Leak

Can you smell gas? Leave the property, don't use any switches, and call us. We are licensed gas fitters with emergency gas detection equipment.

Severe Water Leak

Visible water leaking rapidly from pipes, fixtures, or appliances. Shut off water at the mains if safe, then call us straight away to minimise damage.

No Water at All

A complete loss of water supply (not a street outage) usually means a burst main or failed pressure limiting valve — an urgent fix that we handle 24/7.
